About Me

I've been behind the camera ever since I moved to Ann Arbor in 2007. I developed a particular interest in fashion photography after reading several fashion blogs my friends were into. I really enjoy men's and women's style equally and even like to see them mix when it's done well.

Fashion is an expressive medium and as such offers an individuality many others can't. Each person can take fashion and really make it their own. There are several organizations in Ann Arbor doing just that. Learning what makes people want to design, produce, and wear different materials and patterns is simply intriguing and often insightful.

I'm working slowly on ways to foster the existing fashion and style in Ann Arbor. It's a small town but it's bursting with talent. I can't wait to see it thrive!

I'd like to thank you for your interest in my work. I encourage you contact me about anything from booking to talking shop about fashion!
